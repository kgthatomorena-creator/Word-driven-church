Our Vision

Our Story

Word Driven Church is a church that believes in the Word of God. We are motivated to do everything the Bible says we must do, and condone every wrongdoing according to the Holy Scriptures.

Word Driven Church started in September 2012 by Pastor Aaron Mkhonto and Pastor Dintshi Mkhonto with a Crusade held in a tent at Witbank, Klarinet Extension 6. We had a tent that could only accommodate 50 people maximum. However, by the grace of the Lord, we have moved from Level 1 to another Level, and the Lord our God is still moving us to another dimension.

Previously we were known as Deliverance of Souls Ministries, and in 2017 we changed our name to Word Driven Church, because we believe that any soul can be delivered only if you allow the Word of God to drive your life.

"Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path."

- Psalm 119:105
